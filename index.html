Know Us More

We're The Undesirables, a Gaming Community

Founded in 2017 as a new home for members of RIFM, The Undesirables (UDS) gaming community has been a free and welcoming home to Milsim and general online gamers ever since. .

While our bread and butter is weekly CO-OP Arma missions on our private server every Tueday night, we're more than a one-night-wonder. We've built a lively community where our members have built lasting friendships, sharing laughs and regularly playing games well beyond Arma. We try to give our members as much support in this as possible through discord, private TeamSpeak Server, and using our private server to host other games as the community asks, such as DayZ, DCS, and Valheim.

The Community

What We Do?

Private Server

Not just a discord group for socialising with other gamers, UDS maintains a private gaming server for hosting regular gaming events as well as unofficial "off-night" play. This server is maintained through donations from our members, however membership and play is free and donations are 100% voluntary.

Arma Missions

UDS started as an ARMA Milsim-lite group, and its main event is the weekly Arma 3 Mission night on Tuesday evenings. While numbers can vary, mission nights regularly see 20-30 players come together to execute 2 hour CO-OP missions, set in a variety of eras, and using a wide range of equipment and scenarios. The "lite" in Milsim-lite means that while we try to borrow from real world practices where they make the game more enjoyable, there are no fixed ranks or roles in the community, and no calling anyone "sir".

DCS

Starting as an Arma group brings a certain fondness for military equipment, so its no surprise that some of our members have also gravitate to DCS. When not playing on a variety of public server UDS also runs custom DCS missions and Liberation campaigns

Games of the Day

Our community doesn't begin and end with military sims. Our members have a diverse taste in games and are often keen to jump on multiplayer with friends. To help our members get the most out of the community, we try to support the games of the day by making dedicated servers available on request

We try and keep it simple...

Play as a Team

Being surrounded by like minded friends who all have the same goal is part of what makes playing in a group special. Those friends rely on you to make their night fun as much as you rely on them. Empower your plat, section and fire team by covering your buddies, following orders, and finishing this sentence in an inspiring way.

Fair Play

Whether you’re making mission or playing them, keep the field even for all players, Avoid behaviour that causes an imbalance in mission mechanics, assets or magical outcomes.

Don’t Screw your Crew

Everyone has a right to a good night. Consider other players' enjoyment when you fight by their side, or design missions for them. Players: Master your role, Makers: Be creative and show your style but test your missions so it’s alright on the night.

Be an Agent of Change and Balance

This is your group, and your opinion matters. Help the group evolve by bringing new ideas to the table, but also balance this desire for change by considering the mistakes people have tried to avoid in the past.

By choosing to make a once off or regular donation to The Undesirables running costs, you'll be helping us keep our fascilities open and free for the wider community. In return we'll flag you as a donating member, and also give you a say in what other games we host on our server for member enjoyment on non-mission nights.
